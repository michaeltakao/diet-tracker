Review this product specification, feature, or design for quality.

Evaluate from three angles:

## Customer Perspective
- Does this solve a real, specific problem?
- Would a real customer pay for this?
- What will the customer try first — and why might they abandon it?
- What is missing that customers will ask for on day 1?

## Technical Perspective
- Is this buildable in the stated timeframe?
- What are the technical risks?
- What shortcuts are acceptable at this stage vs. what creates dangerous debt?
- What monitoring / observability is needed?

## Business Perspective
- Does this move the primary metric?
- What is the cost to build vs. expected return?
- Does this create lock-in or is it easily copied?
- What is the opportunity cost — what are we NOT building by doing this?

## Summary

Provide:
1. Critical issues (would cause product failure)
2. Important concerns (would cause significant friction)
3. Minor suggestions
4. Strengths worth keeping

Overall verdict: Ship it / Revise first / Don't build
