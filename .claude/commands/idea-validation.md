Validate this AI service or product idea before any building begins.

Work through each dimension:

## Customer
- Who specifically? (job title, situation, frequency of pain)
- How do you know they have this problem? (evidence / assumption)

## Problem
- What are they doing today instead?
- What is the cost of that workaround? (time / money / frustration)
- How often does this problem occur?

## Competition
- Who already solves this?
- Why would a customer switch?

## Revenue
- Would they pay? How much? (comparable pricing in the market)
- B2B or B2C? Why?

## Feasibility
- Can this be built in 4 weeks to validate?
- What is the core technical risk?

## Risk Matrix
Rate each assumption:
- Customer exists: [High / Medium / Low confidence]
- Problem is painful enough: [High / Medium / Low]
- Willingness to pay: [High / Medium / Low]
- Technical feasibility: [High / Medium / Low]

## Verdict
- Go / No-go / Needs validation
- Cheapest validation before writing code (landing page / user interview / mock demo)
- Kill criteria: if [X] by [date], stop
