Analyze this business idea for viability.

Provide a structured assessment:

## Market
- TAM (total addressable market) — size and evidence quality
- SAM (serviceable addressable market) — realistic target segment
- Growth rate and direction
- Confidence: High / Medium / Low (with reason)

## Competition
- Direct competitors (doing the same thing)
- Indirect competitors (solving the same problem differently)
- "Do nothing" — the always-present alternative
- Key differentiator claimed vs. key differentiator defensible

## Business Model
- How money is made
- Pricing model (subscription / usage / one-time / freemium)
- Rough unit economics (CAC estimate, LTV estimate, payback period)
- Path to first $1 in revenue

## Risks
Rank by: (probability of occurring) × (impact if it does)

1. Highest risk assumption
2. Second highest
3. Third

## Verdict
- Go / No-go / Needs validation
- The single most important thing to validate before committing resources
- Recommended first customer (specific, reachable, willing to pay)

Throughout, label each claim:
- **Evidence** — supported by data
- **Assumption** — reasonable but unverified
- **Speculation** — plausible but uncertain
